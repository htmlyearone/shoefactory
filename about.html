About Us: The World Wide Story

The Shoe Factory was established in 2012. Since then we have become one of the most successful shoe shop in Ireland. We started in a small store in cork ad have in just 2 years we have spread across the country with 36 stores nationwide.

we have a low staff turnover and we were nominated for the best place to work 2013. We deliver the best made shoes to our customers, hand made in Italy and France with only the finest materials. We hope to continue to give the excellent customer service and excellent shoes to our customers on a day to day basis.

Our newest home is on Grafton Street in Dublin City Centre. The newly renovated store is perfect and fits all of our needs and hopefully the needs of our customers.we have just signed an exclusive contract to sell the word famous Andrefus range of shoes. The first store to receive these shoes will be our new home on Grafton street.
